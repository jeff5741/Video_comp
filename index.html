Open Space Authority Video Competition

Coyote Valley

Create a short video highlighting how protected spaces like Coyote Valley connect wildlife, farms, and people — and help our region adapt to climate change.

Themes

Coyote Valley

Explore the importance of preserving this vital open space and wildlife corridor.

Nature in Cities

Show how parks, trees, and urban nature benefit communities and biodiversity.

Farming and Food Systems

Celebrate local farmers and sustainable agriculture in Santa Clara Valley.

Nature-Based Solutions

Show how restoring nature can help address flooding, drought, and climate challenges.

Rules & Timeline

  • Open to youth living in Santa Clara County.
  • Videos must be 1–3 minutes long and submitted in .mp4 format.
  • Deadline: April 2026.

Judging Criteria

Category Points Description
Creativity 25 Original storytelling and unique approach
Message 25 Clarity and connection to OSA themes
Visual Quality 25 Camera work, editing, and audio
Impact 25 Emotional and educational resonance

FAQ

Can I submit with friends?

Yes, group entries are welcome! Just include all names in the form.

Do I need professional equipment?

No — smartphone videos are great. Focus on your message and creativity.

When will winners be announced?

Finalists will be notified by May 2026.
